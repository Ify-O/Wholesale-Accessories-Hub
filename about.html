About Wholesale Accessories Hub

Wholesale Accessories Hub was founded in 2015, born out of a simple but powerful idea: to make quality accessories accessible while supporting everyday financial growth.

What began in a university environment as a small buying and reselling effort quickly grew into something more meaningful. It revealed a real gap, many aspiring entrepreneurs wanted to start businesses, but lacked access to trusted suppliers, consistent quality, and fair pricing.

That realization shaped the direction of the business. It moved beyond selling products into something deeper: building access, opportunity, and trust for people starting their own journeys.

After university, the journey continued through relocation, rebuilding, and early operational challenges. Through every phase, one thing remained constant, integrity, consistency, and customer trust.

By 2020, the business expanded more intentionally in Lagos, leveraging digital platforms like Instagram and WhatsApp. Growth was not driven by advertising alone, but by repeat customers, referrals, and long-term trust.

Today, Wholesale Accessories Hub serves retailers, resellers, and business owners across Nigeria, Ghana, Togo, Cameroon, and international markets.

We specialize in high-quality wholesale accessories including stainless steel jewelry, sterling silver pieces, gold-plated designs, watches, belts, cufflinks, and hair accessories.

Our mission remains simple and consistent: to help entrepreneurs and retailers build sustainable businesses through reliable supply, fair pricing, and quality products they can trust.

At the core of everything we do is a belief that business should create opportunity, and that the right supplier can make the difference between an idea and a thriving business.
