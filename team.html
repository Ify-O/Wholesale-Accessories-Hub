Meet the Founder

The founder of Wholesale Accessories Hub is a purpose-driven entrepreneur whose journey is rooted in resilience, growth, and a strong desire to create opportunities for others.

What began as a small initiative during university has evolved into a growing business that now supports retailers and entrepreneurs across multiple countries. This journey has been shaped by consistency, integrity, and a commitment to delivering value.

She holds a Bachelor's degree in Theatre Arts from the University of Ibadan and a Master's degree in Business Management from EU Business School, Barcelona, Spain.

Beyond business, she is also developing her skills as a cloud professional and software developer, with a strong interest in using technology, AI, and digital tools to help businesses scale faster and operate more efficiently.

She is currently building initiatives focused on empowering women and entrepreneurs to leverage technology for growth, while also supporting communities through mentorship, collaboration, and continuous learning.

Known for her integrity, dedication, and genuine passion for people, she brings both a human and strategic approach to business. Her vision is not only to build a successful brand, but to create lasting impact through opportunity, access, and innovation.
